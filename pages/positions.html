Lab Positions

Graduate Student Positions

  1. AMNR Lab annually recruits two graduate students.
  2. Undergraduate Students with great interests in robotics research, and strong background on automation, control theory and engineering, instrumentation and measurement, mechatronics, computer science, eletronics, etc., are highly ecouraged to apply.
  3. Applicants are expected to be highly self-motivated.
  4. Application is highly competitive. Applicants are encouraged to apply as early as possible.
  5. Please email your CV, transcript and personal statement (listing your reasons for your eligibility to be a graduate student in AMNR Lab) to PI directly by liusong@shanghaitech.edu.cn

研究生招生

  1. AMNR实验室每年招收两名研究生。
  2. 我们欢迎对机器人研究充满兴趣，并具有自动化、控制理论与工程、仪器仪表、机电一体化、计算机科学、电子等相关背景的本科生积极申请。
  3. 申请者应具备较强的自我驱动力。
  4. 申请竞争非常激烈，建议有意者尽早申请。
  5. 请将个人简历、成绩单以及个人陈述（说明你认为自己为何适合成为AMNR实验室的研究生）发送至刘松老师邮箱：liusong@shanghaitech.edu.cn
