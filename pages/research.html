Research Overview

Micro-Nano Robotics:

The AMNR group conducts innovative research on micro-nano robotics, specifically on nano-robotic manipulation inside SEM, and latest non-contact micro-manipulation based on emerging acoustic technology.

Research in AMNR Lab is supported by National Natural Science Foundation of China under Grant 62303321.

AMNR课题组开展微纳机器人领域的创新研究，重点包括扫描电子显微镜（SEM）环境下的纳米机器人操控，以及基于新兴声学技术的最新非接触式微操控方法。该研究获得国家自然科学基金项目（62303321）的资助。

Acoustic Microrobotics:

The AMNR group is also exploring the field of acoustic microrobotics, which utilizes sound waves for the manipulation and control of micro-objects. This research aims to develop non-invasive techniques for various applications, including biomedical devices and micro-manufacturing.

We developed a “2500-channel” holographic acoustic tweezer technology capable of efficiently manipulating objects at the microscopic scale, overcoming the challenge of spatiotemporal super-resolution control in ultrasound.

AMNR课题组还在探索声学微机器人领域，利用声波实现对微观物体的操控与控制。该研究旨在开发用于生物医学器械和微制造等多种应用的无创技术。我们研发了全球首个“2500通道”全息声镊子技术，能够在微观尺度上高效操控物体，并攻克了超声波时空超分辨率调控的难题。
